CHOCOLATE CAKE

=============

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 35 minutes
Servings: 8

INGREDIENTS:

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup unsweatened cocoa powder
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 cup boiling water

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
2. Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans
3. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt
4, Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla
5. Mix on medium speed for 2 minutes
6. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin)
7. Pour into prepared pans
8. Bake for 30-35 minutes
9. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans

NOTES:
- Frost with your favourite chocolate frosting
- Store covered at room temperature
