GARDEN SALAD

============

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 0 minutes
Servings: 4

INGREDIENTS:

- 1 head romaine lettuce
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes
- 1 cucumber
- 1/2 red onion
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- Salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Wash and dry all vegetables
2. Chop lettuce into bite-sized pieces
3. Halve cherry tomatoes
4. Slice cucumber and red onion thinly
5. Combine all vegetables in al large bowl
6. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper
7. Toss dressing with vegetables
8. Serve immediatley

NOTES:
- Add croutons for extra cruch
- Can add feta cheese or olives
