SPAGHETTI CARBONARA

===================

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Servings: 4

INGREDIENTS
- 400g spaghetti
- 150g pancetta or guanciale
- 3 large eggs
- 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
- Black pepper to taste 
- Salt for pasta water


INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil
2. Cook spaghetti according to package directions
3. While pasta cooks, dice pancetta and cook until cripsy
4. In a bowl, whisk eggs and grated cheeses
5. Drain pasta, reserving 1 cup of pasta water
6. Combine hot pasta with pancetta
7. Remove from heat and add egg-cheese mixture
8. Stir quickly, adding pasta water as needed
9. Season with black pepper and serve immediatley


NOTES:

- Do not add cream (traditional carbonara has no cream)
- Work quickly to avoid scrambling eggs
